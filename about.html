THE COMPANY

Pattern dynamics is one of the growing company in Bangalore,who are into a business with global demand for clothing has increased manifolds because of increased population, change of consumer's attitude toward fast fashion, and increase in the average income per head in many countries. This increased demand for clothing is fulfilled by the developing countries, which are now becoming the manufacturing hub for several international fashion brands. Apparel manufacturing is still a labor-intensive process in these developing countries, although there have been several automation available commercially.

Availability of cheap labor and the high initial investment are the causes for which the garment industries are not adopting the technology. However, the global demand for high-quality clothing and stiff competition is now mandating many manufacturers to adopt the automation technology. Through a unique interdisciplinary approach, we can bring a range of innovative possibilities to businesses looking for a change. Our broad range and vast catalogue enable us to examine the possibilities for each client from a number of viewpoints and offer a one-stop-shop experience for our customers.
We use creative approaches which combine the latest thinking with both grounded theoretical knowledge and practical experience.

Our adaptable and quality solution provides customers with measured results in a time frame which is individually tailored to each client.

We offer complete range of services required for any Apparel business internationally. We provide our customers with the best pattern suited to their needs drafted with high precision. Our patterns are exported throughout the world We are committed to customer satisfaction and attention to detail. With round the year working, we make ourselves always available for communication to keep our customers more reliable on us for any professional advice and clarifications.

Since 2013, we have successfully served hundreds of customers in more than 10 countries for our clients who are gold-standard in their respective industries. Experienced in Automation Product and Service implementation. Product and Service with Quality is our motto.
